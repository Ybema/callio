The Problem
If you've written a consortium proposal,
you'll recognise this
These structural challenges appear in almost every Horizon proposal process — regardless of topic or consortium size.
👥

Small core team, hidden inconsistencies

As more partners contribute, only a few people maintain the full structural picture — increasing the risk of gaps no one catches until review.

🧪

Specialised input that's hard to assess

Valuable partner content is added on request, but the core team lacks the subject expertise to align it with the overall proposal logic.

📑

Parallel drafts circulating

Multiple versions evolve simultaneously, making it unclear which logic is current and which changes have actually been incorporated.

⚖️

Work packages evolving independently

Different WP leads write at different levels of detail and interpretation — causing uneven structure and subtle misalignment across sections.

🕐

Late budget changes cascade

Near-submission adjustments force the team to realign text, tasks, and justifications under extreme time pressure — often incompletely.

🎯

Call criteria assumed, not demonstrated

Strong content is present, but alignment with evaluation criteria isn't explicitly visible for reviewers — costing points where it matters most.

⚠️

The proposal becomes structurally fragile — not because the idea is weak, but because complexity is no longer actively managed. Callio keeps the structure under control throughout the entire process.

What Callio Does
Not a one-shot tool.
A proposal development partner.
Callio works in iterations alongside your consortium. Each round builds on the last, keeping the entire team aligned.
📑

Call Analysis

Upload your funding call documents — Callio extracts every requirement, deadline, eligibility rule, and evaluation criterion into a structured summary your entire consortium can work from.

🎯

Idea-to-Call Matching

Bring your project idea or draft concept. Callio maps it against the call's priorities, identifies gaps in alignment, and highlights where your strengths match what the funder is looking for.

🧩

Work Package Development

Build and refine individual work packages with structural checks across the full set — ensuring consistent scope, realistic effort, logical dependencies, and alignment between WPs.

📋

Full Proposal Development

Iterate on the complete proposal through multiple review rounds. Callio scores structure and call alignment independently, tracks what changed, and flags what still needs attention before submission.

💡 Consortium feedback loop: Callio tracks recommendations across iterations. When partners don't implement suggested changes, it flags the gap and asks why — so nothing falls through the cracks.
