## WP Performance Matrix Column Definitions

### 1. ALIGNMENT SCORE (Overall Assessment)
**Definition**: Weighted composite score combining all assessment dimensions

**Calculation Formula**:
```
Alignment Score = (LFA Integration × 30%) + 
                 (EU Excellence × 25%) + 
                 (EU Impact × 25%) + 
                 (EU Implementation × 20%)
```

**Scoring Bands**:
- **90-100%**: EXCEPTIONAL - Ready for submission
- **85-89%**: EXCELLENT - Minor improvements needed
- **75-84%**: GOOD - Focused improvements required
- **65-74%**: FAIR - Significant improvements needed
- **<65%**: POOR - Major restructuring required

---

### 2. LFA INTEGRATION (Logical Framework Alignment)
**Definition**: Measures how well the WP aligns with the project's Logical Framework Analysis

**Assessment Components**:
- **Impact Level** (25%): Contribution to overall project impact
- **Outcome Level** (35%): Mapping to specific LFA outcomes (O1-O6)
- **Output Level** (25%): Alignment between WP deliverables and LFA outputs
- **Activity Level** (15%): Coverage of LFA activities by WP tasks

**Scoring Scale**:
- **90-100%**: Direct, substantial contribution with clear pathways
- **80-89%**: Strong contribution with minor gaps
- **70-79%**: Adequate contribution with some unclear connections
- **60-69%**: Weak contribution, major clarification needed
- **<60%**: Poor or missing connection

---

### 3. EU EXCELLENCE (Scientific/Technical Quality)
**Definition**: Assessment against EU evaluation criteria for scientific excellence

**Evaluation Criteria**:
- **Clarity of Objectives** (33%): SMART objectives, measurability, KPI definition
- **Soundness of Concept** (33%): Methodology robustness, validation approach
- **State of the Art Integration** (34%): Literature review, benchmarking, innovation positioning

**Scoring Scale**:
- **90-100%**: EXCELLENT - Comprehensive and robust
- **80-89%**: GOOD - Strong with minor gaps
- **70-79%**: ADEQUATE - Basic requirements met
- **60-69%**: WEAK - Major improvements needed
- **<60%**: POOR - Insufficient or missing

---

### 4. EU IMPACT (Expected Project Impact)
**Definition**: Assessment of WP's contribution to project impact and market relevance

**Evaluation Criteria**:
- **Contribution to Expected Outcomes** (40%): Strength of contribution to project outcomes
- **Credibility of Impact Pathways** (30%): Realism and validation of pathways to impact
- **Communication & Exploitation** (30%): Strategy for results utilization and dissemination

**Scoring Scale**:
- **90-100%**: EXCELLENT - Strong validated contribution
- **80-89%**: GOOD - Good contribution with minor gaps
- **70-79%**: ADEQUATE - Reasonable contribution
- **60-69%**: WEAK - Limited contribution
- **<60%**: POOR - Unclear or missing contribution

---

### 5. EU IMPLEMENTATION (Execution Quality)
**Definition**: Assessment of WP structure, partner roles, and implementation feasibility

**Evaluation Criteria**:
- **WP Quality & Structure** (40%): Milestone appropriateness, deliverable clarity, task organization
- **Partner Roles & Complementarity** (35%): Partner fit, role clarity, resource allocation completeness
- **Risk Management** (25%): Risk identification, mitigation strategies, contingency planning

**Scoring Scale**:
- **90-100%**: EXCELLENT - Optimal structure and execution plan
- **80-89%**: GOOD - Well-structured with minor optimization needed
- **70-79%**: ADEQUATE - Reasonable structure with some issues
- **60-69%**: WEAK - Poor structure requiring major improvements
- **<60%**: POOR - Inadequate or missing structural elements

---

### 6. STATUS CLASSIFICATION
**Definition**: Overall readiness assessment based on Alignment Score

**Status Categories**:
- **EXCEPTIONAL** (90-100%): Ready for submission
- **EXCELLENT** (85-89%): High quality foundation
- **GOOD** (75-84%): Solid foundation
- **FAIR** (65-74%): Adequate baseline
- **POOR** (<65%): Major gaps identified

---

### 7. PRIORITY ACTIONS
**Definition**: Top 1-2 immediate improvement actions derived from lowest scoring dimensions

**Determination Logic**:
1. Identify lowest scoring dimension for each WP
2. Map to specific improvement actions from detailed analysis
3. Focus on highest impact improvements for overall alignment

**Common Action Categories**:
- External validation strategy
- Structural optimization (milestone/deliverable consolidation)
- Integration enhancement (cross-WP coordination)
- Strategic transformation
- Technical enhancement (methodology strengthening)

---

## Weighting Rationale

**LFA Integration (30%)**: Highest weight ensures project coherence and outcome alignment  
**EU Excellence (25%)**: Core scientific quality assessment  
**EU Impact (25%)**: Market relevance and exploitation potential  
**EU Implementation (20%)**: Execution feasibility and structure quality

---

*This scoring methodology ensures transparent, consistent, and actionable assessment of Work Package quality.*
