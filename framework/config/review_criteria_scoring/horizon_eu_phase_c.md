# Phase C: Full Project Analysis - Review Criteria & Scoring
**Funding Type**: HORIZON Europe  
**Phase**: C (Full Project Analysis)  
**Purpose**: Comprehensive call alignment assessment for submission readiness

## Generic Call Alignment Assessment Framework

### 1. Eligibility & Scope Fit (15%)
**Call Requirements**: Formal eligibility and scope conditions
- TRL ranges compliance
- Eligible activities coverage
- Geographic focus alignment
- Partner types requirements

**Project Assessment**: How project fits these requirements
**Risk Flags**: TRL overstated, activities unclear, scope misalignment

**Readiness Scale**:
- **Level 5**: Perfect eligibility fit, all scope requirements clearly met
- **Level 4**: Strong fit, minor clarifications needed
- **Level 3**: Good fit, some scope elements need strengthening
- **Level 2**: Questionable fit, major gaps in requirements
- **Level 1**: Poor fit, significant eligibility concerns

### 2. Expected Outcomes Alignment (20%)
**Call Requirements**: Expected Outcomes as written in Work Programme
**Project Assessment**: Map project objectives/results/WPs to each EO
**Analysis**: Strong vs weak alignments, missing quantification

**Readiness Scale**:
- **Level 5**: All EOs directly addressed with quantified contributions
- **Level 4**: Most EOs well-addressed, minor quantification gaps
- **Level 3**: Good EO coverage, some evidence missing
- **Level 2**: Weak EO alignment, major evidence gaps
- **Level 1**: Poor EO coverage, insufficient mapping

### 3. Scope Requirements & Specific Tasks (15%)
**Call Requirements**: Mandatory tasks, methodologies, impact pathways
**Project Assessment**: Coverage of cross-cutting issues (SSH, gender, open science)
**Gap Analysis**: Missing elements, insufficient coverage

### 4. TRL & Innovation Ambition (10%)
**Call Requirements**: Required TRL starting point and targets
**Project Assessment**: Identify TRLs of activities and endpoints
**Consistency Check**: Alignment across text/tables, realistic progression

### 5. KPIs & Strategic Priorities (10%)
**Call Requirements**: Extract KPIs from SRIA/Work Programme/Mission docs
**Project Assessment**: Map contributions (quantitative preferred) to each KPI
**Quality Check**: Quantitative vs qualitative contributions

### 6. Business Case & Exploitation (10%)
**Call Requirements**: Market relevance, exploitation pathways, business indicators
**Project Assessment**: Business models, NPV/IRR, exploitation strategies
**Strength Assessment**: Quantification quality, investor logic

### 7. Open Science & Data Management (5%)
**Call Requirements**: FAIR data, EOSC use, open access, DMP
**Project Assessment**: How project addresses these requirements
**Consistency Check**: Alignment across Excellence/Impact/Implementation

### 8. Cross-cutting Aspects (5%)
**Call Requirements**: SSH integration, gender dimension, environmental safeguards
**Project Assessment**: Integration into objectives, WPs, impact pathways
**Quality Check**: Superficial vs meaningful integration

### 9. Consortium Composition (5%)
**Call Requirements**: Multi-actor approach, partner balance, geographical spread
**Project Assessment**: Value chain coverage, role clarity, complementarity
**Coverage Check**: All bases covered, no critical gaps

### 10. Risk Assessment (5%)
**Summary**: Main risks of misalignment
- Overstated TRL claims
- Missing mandatory elements
- Insufficient outcome quantification
- Weak exploitation logic
- Poor consortium fit

## Overall Phase C Readiness

### Submission Readiness Scale
**Calculation**: Weighted average of all 10 criteria

**Final Assessment**:
- **Level 5 (4.5-5.0)**: SUBMIT - Proposal is submission-ready
- **Level 4 (3.5-4.4)**: REFINE - Strong proposal, minor improvements
- **Level 3 (2.5-3.4)**: IMPROVE - Good foundation, focused improvements needed  
- **Level 2 (1.5-2.4)**: REWORK - Significant improvements required
- **Level 1 (1.0-1.4)**: RESTART - Major restructuring needed

## Critical Success Factors
1. **Call Alignment**: Direct response to call requirements
2. **Evidence Quality**: Quantified contributions and credible pathways
3. **Consortium Fit**: Right partners with clear, complementary roles
4. **Innovation Credibility**: Realistic TRL progression with validation
5. **Exploitation Logic**: Clear path from research to market impact
