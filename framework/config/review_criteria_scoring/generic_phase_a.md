# Phase A: Generic LFA Review Criteria
**Phase**: A (Logic Framework Analysis)
**Purpose**: Evaluate an LFA against a selected call document without Horizon-specific assumptions.

---

## 1. Call Alignment (Weight: 30%)

**Key Question**: Does the LFA directly address the selected call's priorities and requirements?

- **CA1** Objectives alignment
- **CA2** Scope fit
- **CA3** Outcomes and impact alignment
- **CA4** Evaluation criteria coverage
- **CA5** Eligibility and formalities
- **CA6** Terminology alignment

---

## 2. LFA Internal Consistency (Weight: 50%)

**Key Question**: Is the logic internally coherent and implementable?

- **LC1** Goal-purpose-outcome coherence
- **LC2** Traceability between outcomes, outputs, tasks, and deliverables
- **LC3** Indicator quality and measurability
- **LC4** Timeline and implementation feasibility

---

## 3. Content Quality (Weight: 20%)

**Key Question**: Is the LFA clear, specific, and ready for proposal drafting?

- **CQ1** Clarity and specificity
- **CQ2** Actionability
- **CQ3** Professional structure and readability

---

## Score Scale

All criteria are scored 0-5:
- **5** Excellent
- **4** Good
- **3** Adequate
- **2** Needs work
- **1** Poor
- **0** Critical gap

Overall score:
`0.30 * CA + 0.50 * LC + 0.20 * CQ`
