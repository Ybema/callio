# Work Package Assessment Criteria - Proposal Framework

## Overview
This document defines the comprehensive assessment criteria used by Claude-4-Sonnet for evaluating Work Package descriptions in funding project proposals. The assessment combines systematic LFA alignment analysis with practical evaluation criteria.

---

## 🤖 **Claude-4-Sonnet Assessment Framework**

### **Multi-Dimensional Assessment Approach**
Claude performs comprehensive analysis across four key dimensions:
1. **LFA Integration Assessment** - Alignment with Logical Framework levels
2. **EU Evaluation Criteria Assessment** - Excellence, Impact, Implementation quality
3. **Practical Assessment** - Strong/weak points with actionable recommendations
4. **Cross-WP Integration Analysis** - Interdependency and coordination evaluation

---

## 📊 **Assessment Dimensions & Scoring**

### **1. LFA Integration Assessment (Scored 0-100%)**

#### **Impact Level Alignment**
- **Assessment Focus**: How each WP contributes to overall project impact
- **Scoring Criteria**:
  - 90-100%: Direct, substantial contribution with clear pathways
  - 80-89%: Strong contribution with minor gaps
  - 70-79%: Adequate contribution with some unclear pathways
  - 60-69%: Weak contribution, major clarification needed
  - <60%: Poor or missing impact connection

#### **Outcome Level Alignment**
- **Assessment Focus**: WP deliverables mapped to specific LFA outcomes
- **Evaluation Elements**:
  - Direct mapping between WP deliverables and LFA outcomes
  - Coverage completeness (% of outcomes addressed)
  - Quality of contribution to each outcome
  - Gap identification for uncovered outcomes

#### **Output Level Alignment**
- **Assessment Focus**: Direct alignment between WP outputs and LFA outputs
- **Evaluation Matrix**:
  - **Direct Alignment**: WP deliverable directly matches LFA output
  - **Partial Alignment**: WP deliverable partially addresses LFA output
  - **No Alignment**: WP deliverable doesn't connect to any LFA output
  - **Quality Assessment**: Rating of how well WP output fulfills LFA requirement

#### **Activity Level Alignment**
- **Assessment Focus**: Coverage completeness of LFA activities by WP tasks
- **Evaluation Metrics**:
  - Coverage Completeness: % of LFA activities addressed by WP
  - Missing LFA Elements: Critical activities not covered by any WP
  - Additional WP Activities: Value assessment of activities not in LFA
  - Task-Activity Mapping Quality: Clarity of connection between WP tasks and LFA activities

#### **LFA Assumptions & Risks Validation**
- **Assessment Focus**: How well WP addresses LFA assumptions and risks
- **Validation Categories**:
  - **Validated**: WP explicitly addresses assumption/risk with clear strategy
  - **Partial**: WP partially addresses with incomplete strategy
  - **Missing**: WP doesn't address critical assumption/risk

### **3. Practical Assessment Framework**

#### **Strong Points Identification** ✅
**Assessment Categories:**
- **Partner Excellence**: "Comprehensive partner mix from packaging to cosmetics"
- **Technical Strength**: "Very strong technical content with clear innovation pathway"
- **Methodological Rigor**: "Strong methodology aligned with CSRD/EU taxonomy"
- **Strategic Alignment**: "Clear objective to translate specs into business case"
- **Coverage Quality**: "Strong coverage of Outcomes 3 & 4, contributes to 5 & 6"

#### **Weak Points Identification** ⚠️
**Assessment Categories:**
- **Structural Issues**: "Too many milestones (11+) - internal checks instead of decision points"
- **Clarity Problems**: "Deliverables read as lists/tables, unclear validation path"
- **Objective Vagueness**: "Objectives vague ('create report') instead of measurable"
- **External Engagement**: "No explicit plan for engaging external buyers beyond consortium"
- **Integration Gaps**: "No description of social/ecological value quantification"

#### **Critical Questions for WP Leads**
**Question Categories:**

**Validation & Methodology Questions:**
1. External validation approach and target stakeholders
2. Quantifiable success indicators and KPIs
3. Methodology robustness and benchmarking approach

**Integration & Coordination Questions:**
4. Cross-WP coordination mechanisms and protocols
5. Duplication avoidance strategies
6. Timeline and deliverable synchronization plans

**Risk & Contingency Questions:**
7. Contingency plans for critical failure scenarios
8. External dependency management strategies

---

### **4. Cross-WP Integration Analysis**

#### **Cross-WP Alignment Matrix**
**Assessment Framework:**
- **LFA Outcome Mapping**: How each WP contributes to each LFA outcome
- **Leading vs Supporting Roles**: Primary responsibility vs supporting contribution
- **Coverage Quality**: Effectiveness of multi-WP outcome coverage
- **Gap Identification**: Outcomes with insufficient WP coverage

#### **Integration Quality Indicators**
- **Good Alignment**: Outcomes covered by 2-3 WPs with clear coordination
- **Critical Gaps**: Outcomes with single WP coverage or missing elements
- **Duplication Risks**: Multiple WPs addressing same elements without coordination
- **Missing Elements**: Critical functionality not covered by any WP

## 🎯 **Assessment Output Framework**

### **Quantitative Scoring**
- **Overall Alignment Score**: Weighted average across all dimensions
- **LFA Integration Score**: Specific score for LFA alignment quality
- **EU Excellence Score**: Specific score for EU evaluation criteria
- **Cross-WP Integration Score**: Specific score for coordination quality

### **Qualitative Assessment**
- **Strong Points**: 3-5 specific strengths with evidence
- **Weak Points**: 3-5 specific weaknesses with improvement recommendations
- **Critical Questions**: 8 structured questions for WP leads
- **Priority Actions**: Top 3 immediate improvement actions

### **Integration Analysis**
- **Cross-WP Matrix**: Visual representation of WP contributions to LFA outcomes
- **Coordination Recommendations**: Specific actions to improve integration
- **External Validation Requirements**: Market, regulatory, stakeholder validation needs

## 📋 **Quality Assurance Standards**

### **Assessment Consistency**
- **Objective Criteria**: Quantitative thresholds for all scoring dimensions
- **Evidence-Based**: All assessments supported by specific examples from WP content
- **Comparative Analysis**: Consistent standards applied across all WPs
- **Actionable Feedback**: All recommendations include specific improvement actions

### **Output Quality Standards**
- **Comprehensive Coverage**: All assessment dimensions addressed for each WP
- **Practical Focus**: Recommendations aligned with real EU evaluation process
- **Integration Emphasis**: Strong focus on cross-WP coordination and LFA alignment
- **Stakeholder Value**: Questions and recommendations valuable for WP leads and coordinators
